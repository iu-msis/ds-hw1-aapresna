Welcome To My Website

This website is all about plane spotting. Plane spotting is the art of locating and recording airplane information. Many "plane spotters" are professional photographers who spend hours at airports photographing airplanes. The most important part of plane spotting is to record the airplane tail number. Every airplane has a unique tail number, similar to a car's license plate. Tail numbers can change depending on who owns the airplane. This is why tail numbers are similar to license plates and not VIN numbers.

On this website, you'll find information about me, a table of my plane spottings, as well as pictures of airplanes that I have spotted. Enjoy!
